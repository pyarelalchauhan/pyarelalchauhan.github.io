I'm moving my writing here, onto my own site, so everything lives in one place and stays under my control.

Going forward, I'll use this space for:

- **Research notes** — things I learn while working on LiDAR point-cloud segmentation, domain adaptation, and urban perception.
- **Practical guides** — reproducible setups for deep-learning workflows (the kind of thing I used to keep on Notion).
- **Project write-ups** — short posts accompanying papers and open-source releases.

## Why a self-hosted blog?

Owning the platform means the content is indexed under my own domain, the formatting is consistent with the rest of the site, and there's no lock-in. Posts are just Markdown files — easy to write, version-controlled in git, and rendered as static pages.

Stay tuned — more posts coming soon.
